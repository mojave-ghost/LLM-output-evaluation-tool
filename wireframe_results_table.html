1
Filter bar: status chips are multi-select toggles. Rubric chip opens a dropdown of saved rubrics. Date inputs are native date pickers. Score range uses a dual-handle slider (1.0–5.0).
2
Bulk action bar slides in above the table when any row checkbox is selected. Replaces the filter bar row to preserve vertical space.
3
Score column: composite shown as a number + 5-dot row. Dots are filled (purple), half-filled (light purple), or empty (gray). Processing/queued rows show a dash — score not yet available.
4
Status badges are color-coded per the design tokens. "failed ×3" badge shows retry exhaustion inline. "cached" badge means result was returned from dedup cache — identical score to an earlier job.
5
Clicking any row (not the checkbox) opens the Result Detail View. Chevron › reinforces this. Rows are keyboard-navigable (↑↓ to move, Enter to open).
