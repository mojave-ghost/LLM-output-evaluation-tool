1
KPI row: four headline numbers — total jobs, mean composite (color-coded by tier), live queue depth, and cache hit rate. Delta indicators show period-over-period change. Time-range pills control all charts simultaneously.
2
Score histogram: bars color-coded by quality tier — red ≤2.0, amber 2.0–3.0, yellow-green 3.0–3.5, teal 3.5–4.5, dark teal 5.0. Visualizes the distribution shape at a glance without reading individual numbers.
3
Dimension averages: per-dimension bar chart with the weighted composite formula shown explicitly below. Surfaces the insight that Correctness drives composite score most — key for rubric design decisions.
4
Trend sparkline: daily mean composite over the selected time window. Area fill under the line. Rightmost point shows current value. All chart data hits GET /api/v1/dashboard.
5
Status donut + queue gauge live-refresh independently: donut every 30 s, queue depth every 5 s. Queue gauge uses a log-scale bar since values are almost always near zero against a 500-cap.
